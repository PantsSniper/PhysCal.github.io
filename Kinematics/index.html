Kinematics

Kinematics is the most basic science of motion. Its equations can describe how fast a sprinter runs a race, how high a ball flies in the air, and how fast a rocket must move to reach Mars in a certain time-frame. This section is most useful in solving problems relating to changes in position or velocity over time.
